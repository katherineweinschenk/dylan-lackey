About Me

Dylan Lackey is a PhD candidate in English and Media Studies at Virginia Commonwealth University, specializing in psychoanalytic theory, Black studies, and critical theory. His dissertation examines the intersection of Lacanian psychoanalysis and Black radical thought. He expects to graduate in May 2025.
